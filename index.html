About Me


The stories I write require imagination. That vision takes form through many artistic mediums. Those drawings, paintings and images imply that there is so much more to convey. Cinematography picks up where they fall off accompanied by music that supports its visual prowess. Sometimes the music generates the very video you observe through code which evokes deeper thought into the beauty programming is capable of. And eventually you explore how the computer can replicate the world around you through mathematics and logic.

My craftsmanship and love for hardware meets my appreciation and understanding of software to form the ultimate artist that uses any tool he can get his hands on to render the canvas. As I come full circle I realise…..I’m broke. So I buckle down and train the skills that are sellable in the job market: programming, data visualization, data science, etc. The same languages I drop inside a raspberry pi to make trippy videos I use to maintain servers and build corporate websites. Couldn’t ask for a better obsession!!!

- Shakwan Yumon Williams
